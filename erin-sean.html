Words from Friends

The relationship that Erin and Sean have created embodies the love, respect, and joy we all hope to find in a partner. The kind of relationship that restores hope and reminds me of the goodness in people and the connection we can create. To be around them is to witness true love and to understand why it’s so rare. I have never met two people better matched for each other and it’s been amazing to watch them grow together, easily navigating the challenges of life. Individually, these two are forces to be reckoned with. Together, they are truly unstoppable.

We want our friends to find partners who not only support and love them but also inspire them to be the best versions of themselves everyday. We see this in Sean and Erin's relationship time and time again. The small, consistent ways we see them care for and serve each other are just as telling as the big gestures and shows of affection. Both Sean and Erin also have the rare balance of being dreamers AND doers. They set lofty goals and support and challenge each other to achieve them without worry of going at it alone. As a couple, the way that they love each other inspires other's loving relationships. Some of this comes from their individual gifts of building community, but together they remind others how to love people generously and well.
