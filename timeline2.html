A timeline of us

You are my sunshine

26 SEPT 2018

It was a rainy fall evening at the Hibernian in Raleigh. After I made an on-stage appearance singing You are My Sunshine with the band, you introduced yourself and we chatted about music and work over the sound of one of our favorite bluegrass songs: Rye Whiskey.

The Beyond

9 OCT 2018

Our first date was at NCMA for The Beyond: Georgia O'Keeffe and Contemporary Art followed by drinks at Boxcar and a walk to the capitol building. Nothing has been more fitting than the words of O'Keeffe that inspired the exhibit that night: "I've been absolutely terrified every moment of my life - and I've never let it keep me from doing a single thing I wanted to do."

Rainy in San Diego

31 DEC 2018

It was my first trip to San Diego and I don't think I had ever been more nervous or excited for anything. I finally got to see your world and meet your people and while the weather was subpar, the people certainly were not.

three little words

14 FEB 2019

We said "I love you" for the first time on Valentine's Day of 2019. I still have the card with the poem you wrote me. You were so excited to give it to me you made me pull over on the way to work and you surprised me in the parking lot at Dix Park. I still love you more than yesterday...and I loved you a lot yesterday.

Taking Flight

9 NOV 2019

I think I may have been more excited about this than you were, maybe because I didn't have to cope with the anxiety of potentially crashing us. You are so fierce in your pursuit of new experiences, even when they come with nerves.

Love Honor Truth

You walked me around your college town and I finally got to see all the places the stories came from. I loved imagining what it must have been like and I'm so thankful for all that you got to experience there because it made you who you are today and I'm so in love with that man.

Happy New Year?

1 JAN 2020

Ringing in the new year with you is one of my favorite memories. An evening at a fancy party with all of our friends followed by a morning of brunch-making and an afternoon in the park. Big moments, small moments and all the ones in between remind me how lucky I am to share this life with you.

27 FEB 2020

Sawadee Ka

All I have to say is I got to go on the trip of my dreams with the man of my dreams. I enjoyed every exhausted minute, every sweaty afternoon, every not-so-spicy panang curry, every beachside pina colada and just about every experience except riding that stupid scooter.

Quarantime on the Road

MARCH-JUNE 2020

I feel confident that if we can be around each other 24/7 for several months, we can handle anything. Not to mention we spent a whole month of that time in a van. From Raleigh to Dallas to Santa Fe to Denver to St. Louis to Nashville to Las Vegas to San Diego, I saw time and time again that you are not only the man of my dreams, but my best friend. You're the best adventure buddy I've ever had even when we're up at 3 a.m. yelling at each other over a couple mice.

Back to my roots

22 AUG 2020

I spent so much of my childhood on the farm in Michigan and it meant the world to me that you wanted to see it and made it happen. I feel more understood and like I can talk about those memories and you get that part of me now.
